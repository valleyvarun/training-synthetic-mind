The aim of my project is to explore and prototype a sketch-based interface for CAD that allows designers to interact with CAD software through drawing.

From my experience in architecture, a lot of time goes into translating design sketches into CAD. If we can speed up this technical step, we can dedicate more time to the artistic aspects of design‚Äîexploring ideas, making creative decisions, and refining our work through iteration.
Tools that explore drawing as a form of interaction
  • SILK lets you sketch user interface elements by hand and makes them interactive.
  • ControlNet generates detailed images by identifying lines in sketches using AI.
  • RoboSketch allows you to draw in 3D space and animate the resulting skeletons.
  • DrawTalking enables users to animate scenes by speaking commands linked to sketches.

12. Capstone Project Idea Outline

My current project explores a sketch-based interface for CAD software, enabling designers to draw freely and have their sketches interpreted into structured CAD geometry and commands. This system is intended to make digital design workflows more intuitive, flexible, and aligned with traditional creative processes.

The prototype will integrate artificial intelligence trained on multimodal datasets‚Äîcombining sketches, CAD files, annotations, and models‚Äîto understand and translate hand-drawn inputs. The aim is to support diverse design fields such as architecture, urban planning, and product design by bridging the gap between informal ideation and formal digital modeling.

The project also investigates ethical dataset creation and digital asset ownership. By incorporating secure, transparent systems for attribution and compensation, it proposes a framework where contributors of training data can retain rights and receive value for their work.
