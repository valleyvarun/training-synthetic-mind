1. Title:
Training the Synthetic Mind: The Future of Multimodal Datasets
2. Research Questions:
  • Why do we need multimodal AI, and what will the datasets used to train them look like?
  • What types of datasets will be needed to train future multimodal AI systems, and how will these differ from the datasets used today?
  • How will digital assets like 3D models, simulation objects, video, images, materials etc and be sourced, structured, and standardized for training AI?
  • Who will own the rights to the data used in training AI, and what systems of attribution and compensation can ensure fair participation?
  • Why is it necessary to rethink data provenance and authorship in an era where AI models are increasingly trained on complex, human-authored content?
  • What are the limitations of current web-scraped datasets, and how can more intentional, collaborative dataset creation practices improve AI performance and equity?
